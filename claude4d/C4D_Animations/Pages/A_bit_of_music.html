A bit of music

 

Usually a pianist hitting the keys produces the music. Here it’s the music that makes the keys move (no keyframing).

C4D R11.5 - Sound Effector - Top keyboard with Cloner Object  Bottom keyboard with my own filters and bandwidth for every of the 88 notes - A lot of XPresso and C.O.F.F.E.E. For pure sine waves it works well but as there are overtones (harmonic) the result is not exactly what a real musician would be playing.     Using midi files (here .aif or .wav) would be the way to go for a realistic result.
