Virtual Telescope v1.0.3

 

This database containing all the stars from the Hipparcos, Yale Bright Star, and Gliese catalogs (119,613 stars)

All stars have both an epoch and equinox of J2000.0


Pinch to zoom in and out. Pan to move the whole field.

Tap on a star to get all the information about it.

Use the 4 arrows to fine-tune the position.
