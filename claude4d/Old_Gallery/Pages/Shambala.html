Shambala

 
 

In Tibetan Buddhist tradition, Shambala is a mythical kingdom hidden somewhere beyond the snow peaks of the Himalayas. It is mentioned in various ancient texts, including the Kalachakra Tantra and the ancient texts of the Zhang Zhung culture which predated the Tibetan Buddhism in western Tibet. The scriptures speak of a closely related land called Olmolunggring.
