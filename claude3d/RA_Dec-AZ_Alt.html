Right Ascension / Declination

to 

Hour Angle / Declination and Azimuth / Altitude

v1.1.0

 

To do the calculations this application needs your location (longitude and latitude).

Just tap on “Allow” when your start the application the first time.


Then using the keypad enter the position (Right Ascension and Declination) of the object you want to observe.


If your telescope has an equatorial mount you need the Hour Angle and Declination results.


If your telescope has an azimuthal mount you need the Azimuth and Altitude results.

Be aware that in this version the results are without correction for the atmospheric refraction.


If enough people show interest to have a version with that correction I will be pleased to build it. Leave your comments or requests here or on the App Store.


Privacy : No personal data of any kind is collected by this application.
