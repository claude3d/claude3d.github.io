Claude3D is essentially, me.  I started my career as Micro Créatif Inc, in 1980. At first, writing video games for the Apple II and then moved to writing mainly software for 2D digital artists, but not exclusively. Other fields : robotics, camera dolly control, telecommunication, education and finance.


Then a few years at “Formic Videotex System” as VP and Director of Research.


In 1990 I moved to Asia (Japan, Vietnam, China and again Japan) to teach English as a second language to children.


In my spare time in the last three years in Japan, I started to play with Poser, Carrara, modo and Vue. In 2009 I decided it was time to come back to Canada and continue where I had left my previous career. But this time in 3D. I  hesitated between Maya and Cinema 4D, but I finally decided to go with the Cinema 4D R11Studio Bundle.


As I have a BSc in Physics, I was quite pleased when Maxon released R11.5 with Mograph 2.


Presently Cinema 4D is my main tool. I also use Vue xStream within Cinema 4D.


Please feel free to browse around my site and if you see anything of interest, please contact me and I will respond quickly to your request.


Sincerely,


Claude Johnson


Update (Nov 9th, 2010) : I’m back in education. This time in Vietnam. I’m a 1st grade homeroom teacher. Really hard work but I love it. But I still do 3D on my free time.
