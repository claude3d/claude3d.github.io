Cinema 4D - Lights for the thrusters (Reaction Control System RCS)

The Space Shuttle comes in from a lower altitude orbit, so faster and catching up on the ISS. By firing briefly it’s rear thrusters it pushes it into a higher orbit. After a few burns and a few orbits (to get a circular orbit) it reaches the ISS. The docking is achieved by orbital maneuvering, not by direct translation from one point to another. The last adjustments are made using the RCS (Reaction Control System)
